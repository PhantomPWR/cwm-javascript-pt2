Inheritance

When to Use Inheritance

  • When you have two objects with similar properties or methods
  • When you have two objects with a parent-child relationship
  • Use inheritance when you have a "is-a" relationship
  • Use composition when you have a "has-a" relationship
  • Favour composition over inheritance
